剑七中含陌生词的句子

1 use light to help them find prey and aoid obstacles.猎物；障碍物

2 Given that there is…考虑到

3 It is probable that the nocturnal trades go way back in the ancestry of all mammals.夜间的；祖先；哺乳动物

4 It is obstructed and scattered by the dirt in the water.障碍；分散

5 Given the questions of how to manoeuvre in the dark 操控

6 The first one that might occur to him is to manufacture light 生产、制造

7 This doesn’t require a prohibitive amount of energy大量

8 Since the eyes have to detect the tiny fraction of the light that bounces off each part of the scene分数，部分；弹跳

9 Blind humans sometimes seem to have an uncanny sense of obstacles in their path.精准的

10 like the referred pain in a phantom limb幽灵；肢

11 The Sonar and Radar pioneers didn’t know it then声呐，在当时

12 and their ‘radar’ achieves feats of detection and navigation that would strike an engineer dumb with admiration 导航；哑巴
