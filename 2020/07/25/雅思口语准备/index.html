part 1

1

How often do you find time to relax?
通常我都会give myself a short vacation 在双休日，因为我是一名大学生并且在双休日没有课。但是，在exam week，我可能没有时间rest

What’s your ideal form of relaxation?
我不喜欢吵闹的环境，因此对于我来说，我prefer to泡一杯茶在星期六的下午并且读书躺在沙发上，我认为这很pleasant。

What activities did you do as a child to stay healthy?
我居住在宁波，which is a 沿海城市，因此大部分人have been taught to swim在很小的时候。我会back stroke和butterlfy stroke，这对我很有好处。

How healthy do you think your diet is? Why?
我认为我的饮食不太健康。因为我的作业很多，有时候会忘记时间，并且我不会做饭，经常要Order takeaway，很多外卖食物are fried foods with high fat.

What’s your favorite snack between meals？
我喜欢吃饺子，这是一种传统食物并且人们通常会在春节吃它。the outside of the饺子 is a layer of flour，并且里面有各种各样的肉fillings inside.

part 3

1

What do you think fascinates humans about outer space?

Do you think that’s why some stories about space travel have been so ‘imaginative’?

How do you think people’s attitudes towards space exploration have changed since the first Moon landings?

To what extent do you think governments will continue to fund projects in search of life on other planets?

你可以看到很多人都是 big fans of movie which escape from reality. 人们总是attreacted to the unknown，并且他们有强烈的curiosity 去探索 the mystery.

当然，就像我说的，一些关于太空的故事，例如，Interstellar，the wandering earth，both depict the universe in the eyes of mankind.人类 are eager to 认识宇宙，通过 continuous 科学的发展.

自从the first Moon landings，人们变得越来越 enthusiastic 关于太空，我认为这种态度affected一些发展of industries，例如，大学offer aerospace major；研究of materials science 变得很重要。

我认为这取决于economic situation of 各国，如果经济发展好,政府将会 will invest considerable funds to compete for 太空资源, 例如， launching satellites and building space stations.如果经济不好，政府won’t even 花任何钱。

2

What qualities do you think you need in order to be selected for a space mission?

What considerations have to be made before sending humans into space?

To what extent is it better to use robots rather than humans to explore space?

有两个qualities 我必须mention，最重要的品质是morality，一个国家花费了大量资源去cultivate astronauts 我们不能做有害的事to human beings.另一个是physical quality，我们必须有强壮的身体来suit外太空environment。

当我们sending humans into space时，我们必须strictly check whether 火箭系统 is working properly和are astronauts make a full preparation physically and mentally.

On the one hand，这可以避免accidents，maybe save lifes,On the other hand，robots 不会感到 nervous，这increase the success rate.

Why do countries value their beautiful landscapes and wildlife?
一方面，可以增加tourism income，另一方面，可以resist wind。保护野生动物有利于food chain stable。

What disadvantages does tourism bring to these places?
游客可能会破坏自然景观，例如在树上写字，picking leaves，littering everywhere。动物会被disturbed，and forced to leave 这个地方。

How do adults and children differ in the way they experience places of natural beauty?
儿童会被attracted by the 自然美景，例如clear lakes，飞翔的蝴蝶，五颜六色的花，他们会花更多的关注在自然itself。作为对比，成年人will think about the meaning behind scenes，例如它的历史，它的原因of formation。

What can individual do to help protect areas of natural beauty？

When we eat, try to avoid using disposable chopsticks; when we go shopping, try to use cloth bags; when we go to work or school, try to drive as little as possible
